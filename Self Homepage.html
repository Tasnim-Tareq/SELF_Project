About Us

'SELF' represents Love. Respect. Identity .It is a website for the women to realize her dignity, love, passion, respect to herself; a website to encourage women empowerment in our society.
The main focus of our website is to help underprivileged women who are deprived of the blessings of internet. It is a website where a women can share her problems, innovations, creative ideas with us. There are also other facilities including some health campaign, seminar, conference with special supervisors and much more. Campaigns like blood donations, free check up, free schooling for illiterate women.
Seminars for any sort of mental and health problems.One to One counseling. Vocational courses for making you self depended and marketing advertisements of your handmade products to earn something for your livelihood.
There are some NGO's who are working with us hand by hand in order to developing the position of women in our country.
